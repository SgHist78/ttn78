Ghosts ?

An unusual incident, early 1950s

  • Early one morning, P C's eldest son went downstairs to collect the newspapers
  • The way to the outer door was blocked by a wooden latch
  • This was unusual. Never happened before
  • While trying to remove the wooden beam, he heard voices from the staircase top
  • Suddenly, there was a big stomp on the upper stair
  • The boy jumped, surprised, shocked
  • The voices laughed
  • The boy swung round, headed quietly towards the stair
  • Then he saw the grandfather clock. The time was around 1:30 am, not 6 am as thought
  • The boy thought the voices and stomp came from relatives upstairs
  • He moved quietly towards the stairs.
  • If anyone upstairs tried to walk or run away, he could hear it, given the total silence
  • Near the top, the boy rushed up.
  • Nobody ! Total silence.
  • The ensuing days, nobody bragged or talked about it.
  • Ghosts ?
